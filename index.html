2012 VFA-2 Blue Jacket of the Year
Previous Next

The Bluejacket of the Year is an annual award presented by some commands in the U.S. Navy. It is a time-honored Naval tradition similar to an employee of the year award in the civilian world. It is awarded to the Sailor who embodies the Navy core values of Honor, Courage and Commitment and who goes above and beyond the call of duty.

In the F/A-18 squadron VFA-2, the Bluejacket of the Year Award is coupled with a backseat ride in one of the fighter jets. Truly, a once in a lifetime experience.
